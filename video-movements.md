# SKILL: AI VIDEO CAMERA MOVEMENTS (TECHNICAL PROMPTS)
Usa queste stringhe esatte per generare i prompt visivi.

## PAN & TILT (Rotazioni)
- **Pan Right/Left:** "pan right/left. smooth constant rotation. horizon level."
- **Whip Pan:** "whip pan right/left. rapid rotation, fast snap with brief motion blur."
- **Tilt Up/Down:** "tilt up/down. rotate camera upward/downward from fixed point."

## ZOOM (Lenti)
- **Slow Zoom In/Out:** "slow zoom in/out. slowly change lens focal length, gradual and even."
- **Crash Zoom:** "crash zoom in/out. snap the lens rapidly, fast and punchy."

## TRACKING (Spostamenti Fisici)
- **Dolly In/Out:** "dolly in/out. move camera physically forward/backward in a straight line."
- **Truck Right/Left:** "truck right/left. move camera physically to the side on a straight horizontal path."
- **Orbit:** "clockwise/counterclockwise orbit. circle around the subject, background rotates."
- **Drone/Crane:** "crane up/down. travel smoothly upward/downward through open space."

## ISTRUZIONI PER CLAUDE
Per ogni scena dello script, scrivi il prompt visivo in INGLESE così:
`Visual Prompt: [Stringa Tecnica sopra], [Descrizione Soggetto], [Illuminazione], 4k, highly detailed.`
